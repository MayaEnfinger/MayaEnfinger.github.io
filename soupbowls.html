Soup Bowls

This illustration was made in Adobe Fresco. It was an assignment for my digital illustration class where we had to read an article called “The Joylessness of Cooking” and draw a spot, half-page, and full-page illustration. This is the full page illustration, and I wanted to convey the feelings of drowning or becoming so overwhelmed with cooking that was talked about in the article. I wanted the colors and textures to create contrast to the themes so that it was visually more engaging and inviting
