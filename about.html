About Me

My name is Maya Enfinger, and I have been doing some form of art most of my life. I have always had the passion to create and design things, whether it be designing original loom bracelet designs for YouTube, learning to play guitar and songwriting, or teaching myself how to tattoo. From exposure and experience in different creative media, I have found that I can apply what skills, revelations, and principles about design and art to the medium I am currently working in. I have fostered my interest and capabilities in art and design by dedicating myself to the pursuit of creative endeavors, and by doing so I have a clearer idea of the kind of art I want to make. I am working under the name Sapphire Rose because, before I decided to seriously pursue art, I wanted to be a musician, and that was my stage name. I felt it seemed important to keep that name.
