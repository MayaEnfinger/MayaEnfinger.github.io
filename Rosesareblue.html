Roses are Blue

This design was intended as a traditional-inspired tattoo, but it could even exist on clothing. The subject matter was chosen to stay consistent with traditional tattoos, and the handwritten letters are done in the same style for the same reason. The roses are blue is making reference to the roses are red, violets are blue poem, but I changed it, and they are also done in blue because of the name I’ve been publishing art online under, Sapphire Rose.
