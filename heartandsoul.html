Heart and Soul

This poster was made as my choice for a “showpiece” to complement my final presentation in my History of Graphic Design class. I decided to create and design posters in the Fillmore poster style to convey an understanding of the style. This piece was my choice to create, so I had a lot of fun with it. I knew I wanted to create an experience, and I decided that I wanted to create the psychedelic trippy experience that these posters were known for being popular for. I wanted it to feel as if the viewer was taking hallucinogenic drugs. I had to teach myself how to use Adobe After Effects to create this. I made this poster as if I were performing one of my songs, Heart and Soul, as Sapphire Rose.
