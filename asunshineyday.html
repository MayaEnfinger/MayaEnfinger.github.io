A Sunshiney Day

This animation was made in Procreate and Procreate Dreams. This was made for an assignment in my 2D Digital Animation class to animate a tree growing. I decided to draw in a really elementary style using oil pastel brushes to recreate a child’s crayon drawing on the fridge because I wanted to remove the block of immediately achieving perfection and focus on simplicity in order to practice animation.
