UFO Caught on Tape

This simple animation was made in Procreate and Procreate Dreams. I have been using Procreate to draw illustrations, and I recently started using Procreate Dreams to animate. This was made for my 2D animation class with the intention of making a spaceship move across the screen. I made the alien, the spaceship, and the landscape with shades of pink, and I wanted to keep it fun by having the alien do a peace sign at the end. I like doing illustrations that utilize texture brushes to create a different texture.
