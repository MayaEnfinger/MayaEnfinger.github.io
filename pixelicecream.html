Pixel Icecream

This little animation was made in Procreate. I really love how it turned out. I made it one afternoon, just sitting in my boyfriend’s garage, playing with the pixel brush and wanting to just see what happened. There was no real intention or expectation for any specific result. It was just a playful and low-pressure experience, and I learned that it’s okay to embrace simplicity and to just create, and that refinement can happen later.
